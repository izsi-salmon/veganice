Our story

In the begining, Veganice was just a simple ice cream store based in Oriental Parade, selling ordinary ice cream and doing ordinary things. But after the rise in popularity of veganism and a few farming documentries later, we realised we did not want to support that industry anymore. We decided to support the vegan communnity – and the animals! – and turn fully vegan.

What an awesome desiscion! Our little business doubled almost instantly and after lots of hard work and creativity the vegan vibe has spread to three new stores across Wellington and even down to Nelson. Our goal is to spread the vegan message by showing the incredible things that can be created without cruel farming.

We now offer so many exciting flavours and have new ones coming every week. All our ice cream is hand made with whole vegan ingedients. So come down and enjoy a treat that's better for you and better for the planet. Saving the world might not be a piece of cake, but it sure is a scoop of ice cream!
